### Specific Disclaimers

**Nutrition Information:**
- Nutritional estimates (calories, macros, vitamins, etc.) are AI-generated approximations based on image analysis and may not reflect actual nutritional content

- Interaction information is based on publicly available data and AI analysis

  - Newly approved medications
  - Over-the-counter drugs and supplements
  - Herbal remedies

- **ALWAYS consult your doctor, pharmacist, or healthcare provider** before making dietary changes while taking medications

**Restaurant Safety Information:**
- Health inspection scores and safety information may be outdated or incomplete

## 6. Medical Emergencies

### Allergic Reactions

If you experience symptoms of an allergic reaction (hives, swelling, difficulty breathing, anaphylaxis):

1. **CALL 911 or your local emergency number immediately**
2. Use your epinephrine auto-injector (EpiPen) if prescribed
3. Do NOT rely on FCP for emergency medical guidance

### Food Poisoning

If you experience severe symptoms (persistent vomiting, bloody stool, high fever, dehydration):

1. **Seek immediate medical attention**
2. Report foodborne illness to local health authorities
3. Do NOT use FCP as a substitute for medical evaluation

### Drug Interactions

If you experience adverse effects after consuming food while on medication:

1. **Contact your doctor or poison control immediately**
2. In the U.S., call Poison Control: 1-800-222-1222
3. Do NOT rely on FCP for emergency toxicology guidance
